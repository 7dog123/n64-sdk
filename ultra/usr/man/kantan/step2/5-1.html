2.5.1 N64 Sample Programs

There are five samples included with this manual including two graphics samples, two audio samples, and a scheduler sample. Each sample is described within appropriate parts of the N64 Introductory Manual.

Graphics Drawing Samples

  • A 2D image drawing sample is available in the CD's sample directory at:

    \N64Manuals\pc\allman\samples\2d
  • A 3D image drawing sample is available in the following directory on the same CD that holds this manual: 
    \N64Manuals\pc\allman\samples\3d

Audio Playing Samples

  • A sample showing how to reproduce sound effects is available in the CD's sample directory at: 
    \N64Manuals\pc\allman\samples\Effect
  • A sample showing how to reproduce a MIDI sequence is available in the CD's sample directory at: 
    \N64Manuals\pc\allman\samples\Sequence

Scheduler Sample

  • A sample showing how to effectively mix graphics and audio is available in the CD's sample directory at: 
    \N64Manuals\pc\allman\samples\Mix
