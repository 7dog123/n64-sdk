2.3.2 Hardware Processes

CPU Process

The CPU completes these steps:

  1. Construct the audio command list in RDRAM for waveform synthesis.
  2. Transfer the audio command list and audio microcode to the RSP.
  3. Transfer the waveform data (linear PCM) from the RSP to RDRAM to the AI.

RSP Process

The RSP completes these steps:

  1. Execute the microcode transferred from RDRAM.
  2. Accept the audio command list in RDRAM and synthesize the waveform data.
  3. Transfer the synthesized waveform data to the audio buffer in RDRAM.
