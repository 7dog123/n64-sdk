Purpose

1. What is the MONEGI Multi Viewer 64?

The MONEGI Multi Viewer 64 (hereinafter, MMV64) is a viewer for the Nintendo 64 (hereinafter, N64).

The MMV64 can be associated with various types of files.

The following can be used as target N64 devices.

  • MONEGI Smart Pack A,B,C
  • Partner-N64PC
  • IS-Viewer64

2. Operating Environment

MMV64 will operate under the following environments.

  • OS

    • Windows 95/98/NT Ver. 4.0 or later

  • Hardware

    • CPU: Pentium 200MHz or faster is recommended
    Memory: 64MB or more is recommended
    N64: MONEGI Smart Pack A,B,C or Partner-N64PC (ISA) or IS-Viewer64

    Note: The PCI version of Partner-N64PC cannot be used. Partner-N64PC cannot be selected under Windows NT.
