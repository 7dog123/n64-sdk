Function
nuAuSeqPlayerFadeOut
Syntax
void nuAuSeqPlayerFadeOut(u32 player_no ,u32 speed)

Arguments

  • player_no is the sequence player number

  • speed is the time until the sound has gone (number of frames)

Return Value
None

Explanation
Fade out the sequence play back for player which is specified by sequence player number. Fade out gradually over the specified time (number of frames) which is specified by speed, and complete the sequence play back when the volume reaches zero.
