How to Play How to play Snake Tail Hack.

Taking tails

To take tails from the opponents, you place your head on opponent's tail and press the A Button. This results in you taking the opponent's tail segment that you placed your head on and all the tail segments that follow it.

- Put your head on opponent's tail - Take the tail segments by pressing the A button!

Firing tail

By pressing the A Button, you can fire a tail segment forward. You can continue to fire as long as you have tail segments.

By hitting your opponent's head with a tail segment, you can make the opponent faint for 2 seconds.

- One tail segment will come off when firing. -Faint! The fired tail segment hits opponent's head.

Fired tail

A fired tail segment goes straight until it hits an obstacle. An obstacle means a wall, a tail segment, or a head.

It is possible to protect against an opponent's fired tail segment using your tail. In order to fire a tail segment, your tail must not be in the direction of the head.

Time restriction

The time restriction for this game is 100 seconds.

Winning conditions

When the following conditions are met, a winner is determined.

Got 20 tails.

When timeout occurs, the opponent having the most tail segments wins.

In the following case, the game is a draw.

When timeout occurs, the number of tail segments are equal.
