Audio Interface

Access to the audio subsystem is provided through the functions in the Audio Library. The Audio Library supports both sampled sound playback for sound effects and wavetable synthesis from MIDI files for background music. For more information about the Audio Library, please refer to Section 17, “Audio Library.”
